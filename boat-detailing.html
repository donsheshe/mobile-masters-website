What We Do

Marine Detailing Services

From runabouts to cruisers, we restore and protect your vessel — wherever it's berthed across Perth and Mandurah.

⚓
Hull Polish & Cut

Machine cut and polish to remove oxidation, scuffs and waterline staining — restoring deep gloss to gelcoat and topsides.

🪵
Teak Restoration

Cleaning, brightening and oiling of teak decks and trim to bring back natural colour and protect against the elements.

🧪
Tannin & Stain Treatment

Acid treatment for tannin staining from the Swan River and Peel Inlet, plus rust and waterline stain removal.

✨
Gelcoat Oxidation Removal

Multi-stage compounding and polishing to revive chalky, faded gelcoat on hull and superstructure.

🛡️
Marine Ceramic & Sealant

Marine-grade ceramic coating and sealants for lasting UV, salt and stain protection — less cleaning between trips.

🧽
Interior & Cabin Detail

Full interior clean — vinyl, upholstery, carpets and compartments — for a fresh, hygienic cabin.
