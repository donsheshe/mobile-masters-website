Red Dirt Removal

Just back from the Kimberley, Pilbara, Gibb River Road or the Nullarbor? WA red dirt doesn't wash off — it embeds. We remove it properly with iron decontamination and clay bar extraction. Caravans, 4WDs, boats and trailers.

Why Washing Doesn't Work

The Problem With WA Red Dirt

WA red dirt contains fine iron oxide particles that penetrate gelcoat, fibreglass, rubber seals and checkerplate under Perth's UV and heat. Once embedded, they can't be rinsed away.

⚠️Don't scrub it off. Aggressive scrubbing without iron decontamination drives particles deeper and causes micro-scratches that permanently dull fibreglass finishes.
Our Process

How We Remove It

Step 01
Iron Decontamination Wash
A specialist iron fallout remover chemically releases iron particles from the surface — safe for all gelcoat and paint finishes.
Step 02
Clay Bar Extraction
A professional clay bar physically extracts remaining particles, leaving the surface perfectly smooth and contamination-free, ready for polish and protection.
Step 03
Machine Polish
We inspect under light for remaining staining, then machine polish to restore gloss and clarity to the gelcoat or paintwork.
Step 04
Protective Sealant
A UV-resistant sealant or ceramic coating protects against future contamination and makes cleaning far easier after your next outback run.
Step 05
Interior Red Dirt Treatment
Red dirt gets inside too. We extract contamination from fabrics, carpet, upholstery and compartments with specialist equipment — not just a vacuum.

FAQ

Common Questions

What makes WA red dirt different to regular dirt?+
WA red dirt contains fine iron oxide particles that bond to gelcoat, fibreglass and rubber under heat and UV. Unlike regular dust which rinses off, iron oxide chemically adheres — specialist iron decontamination is required to break that bond safely.
Can I just wash it off myself?+
You can remove surface dust with a good wash, but embedded particles need chemical decontamination to release safely. Scrubbing without the right products scratches the surface and drives particles deeper.
How long does red dirt removal take?+
A standard caravan takes 3–6 hours depending on contamination level. A van that's been sitting for weeks takes longer than one brought in promptly after the trip.
Do you treat the inside too?+
Yes — red dirt gets into interior fabrics, carpet, upholstery and compartments. We include specialist extraction cleaning for all fabric surfaces and compartments.
What about my 4WD and trailer?+
Absolutely — we treat cars, 4WDs, trailers, boats and caravans. We can quote and treat the full rig in one visit.
