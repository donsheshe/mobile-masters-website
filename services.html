What We Specialise In

OUR DETAILING SERVICES

Professional mobile detailing for caravans, motorhomes, and cars — across all of Perth. Every service is performed by Mobile Masters Detailing, at your location, with professional-grade products.

Service 01

CARAVAN DETAILING

Perth's UV and heat is relentless — and caravans take the full brunt of it. Whether your van has just returned from a Pilbara adventure or been sitting in storage all winter, Mobile Masters Detailing brings it back to life at your location.

  • ✓Full exterior wash, clay bar, polish and seal
  • ✓Interior deep clean — kitchen, bathroom, upholstery
  • ✓Roof clean and UV treatment
  • ✓Black streak removal and prevention
  • ✓Slide-out seals cleaned and conditioned
  • ✓Awning clean and treatment
  • ✓Fibreglass oxidation restoration
  • ✓All van types — pop-tops, fifth-wheelers, motorhomes

Service 02

BOAT & MARINE DETAILING

Professional mobile boat detailing at your marina, mooring, driveway or storage. Hull cleaning, gelcoat restoration, ceramic coating and trailer protection — we come to you — just provide access to a water tap and power point.

  • ✓Full hull wash, waterline scrub and decontamination
  • ✓Acid treatment for tannin staining (Swan River, Peel Inlet)
  • ✓Gelcoat oxidation removal and machine polish
  • ✓Teak deck cleaning and oil treatment
  • ✓Stainless steel polish and protective coating
  • ✓Ceramic marine coating — hull and topside
  • ✓Boat trailer salt flush and rust protection

Service 03

RED DIRT REMOVAL

Just returned from the Kimberley, Pilbara, Gibb River Road or the Nullarbor? Red dirt embeds deep into gelcoat, seals, awnings and checkerplate. We remove it properly — not just rinse it off.

  • ✓Specialist red dirt decontamination wash
  • ✓Clay bar treatment to extract embedded dirt from gelcoat
  • ✓Full exterior polish and protective seal after removal
  • ✓Seals, vents and checkerplate detail
  • ✓Awning and roof rack clean
  • ✓Available for caravans, boats, trailers and 4WDs

Service 05

CERAMIC COATING

The ultimate long-term protection for your caravan or car. A professionally applied ceramic coating bonds to the surface and creates a hydrophobic, UV-resistant barrier that makes cleaning easier and keeps your finish looking newer for longer.

  • ✓Professional-grade ceramic coating application
  • ✓Surface preparation and paint correction prior to coating
  • ✓Hydrophobic finish — water, dirt and grime repels easily
  • ✓UV and oxidation protection — critical in Perth's climate
  • ✓Available for caravans, motorhomes, and cars
  • ✓Long-lasting results — years, not months

Service 05

PAINT CORRECTION

Swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation can make even a newer van look tired. Machine polishing removes these imperfections and restores the true depth and gloss of your paintwork or gelcoat.

  • ✓Machine polish to remove swirls and light scratches
  • ✓Gelcoat restoration for fibreglass panels
  • ✓Oxidation removal and surface restoration
  • ✓Finishing polish for maximum gloss and depth
  • ✓Always followed by protective sealant or coating

Service 05

INTERIOR DETAILING

A spotless interior makes every trip better. We deep clean every surface — from the roof lining to the floor, including the kitchen, bathroom, and all storage areas.

  • ✓Full vacuum throughout — including under seats and cushions
  • ✓All hard surfaces cleaned and sanitised
  • ✓Kitchen benches, cupboards, and appliances cleaned
  • ✓Bathroom and toilet area fully cleaned and deodorised
  • ✓Upholstery shampoo and fabric protector (premium packages)
  • ✓Pet hair removal available as add-on
  • ✓Odour treatment available
