FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

Do I need to supply water or power?+
Yes — we use your water and power supply. Please ensure there is access to an outdoor tap and a standard 240V power point near where the vehicle is parked. This is standard for all mobile detailing services.
How long does a caravan detail take?+
Typically 4–8 hours depending on size and condition. We give you an accurate time estimate with your quote before we start.
Do you service boats and marine vessels?+
Absolutely — hull polish, teak restoration, acid treatment for tannin staining (Swan River, Peel Inlet), and marine-grade protection for all vessel types.
What is red dirt removal?+
WA red dirt embeds deep into gelcoat, seals, awnings and checkerplate. We use specialist iron decontamination and clay bar treatment to properly extract it — not just rinse it off.
Are you insured?+
Yes — fully insured for mobile detailing work across all Perth metro and surrounding areas. Certificate of insurance available on request.
Do you offer ceramic coating?+
Yes — ceramic coating is available for caravans, motorhomes and cars. Surface preparation and paint correction are included prior to application.
