Caravan Detailing Cost in Perth: A 2026 Price Guide

"How much to detail my caravan?" is the question we hear most. The honest answer is: it depends on a few things. Here's a clear breakdown so you know what to expect before you book — in Perth, in 2026.

What Affects the Price

  • Size. A pop-top is quicker than a 22-foot family van or a motorhome.
  • Condition. A maintained van that's washed regularly costs less than one with heavy oxidation, black streaks or embedded red dirt.
  • Package. A maintenance wash is very different from a full cut-polish-and-ceramic-coat.
  • Extras. Roof oxidation removal, awning cleaning, interior deep-clean and red dirt decontamination add time.

Typical Perth Price Ranges

As a guide, mobile caravan detailing in Perth generally falls into three tiers:

  • Maintenance detail — from $250. Full exterior wash, wheels, windows, interior vacuum, awning rinse and tyre dressing. Ideal between trips.
  • Full detail — from $450. Adds clay bar, UV protectant, sealant, roof treatment and a full interior deep clean. Our most popular option.
  • Showroom detail — from $750. Paint correction, ceramic coating, upholstery shampoo and full restoration.

You can see exactly what's included at each level on our packages page.

What Should Be Included

A quality detail is more than a wash. Look for clay-bar decontamination, UV protection chosen for WA conditions, attention to seals and checkerplate, and an honest assessment of whether your gelcoat needs correction. Cheapest isn't always best — a $120 "wash and go" won't protect your van through a Perth summer.

DIY vs Professional

You can absolutely maintain your van yourself between details. But paint correction, ceramic coating and proper iron decontamination need specialist products and machine polishing — done wrong, they can damage the finish. For those jobs, a professional pays for itself in protection and resale value.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to detail a caravan in Perth?+
Most caravan details range from around $250 for a maintenance wash to $750+ for a full paint-correction and ceramic-coating package, depending on size and condition. A firm quote is given before any work starts.
Why is caravan detailing more than a car?+
Caravans are larger, have more surface area, awnings, roof areas, seals and checkerplate, and often more oxidation from sun exposure — so they take longer than a standard car detail.
Do you come to me?+
